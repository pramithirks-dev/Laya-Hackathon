The Challenge

Why This Matters

Laya Healthcare processes 35,000 claims/month with fragmented manual workflows

The "Black Box" Submission

Members submit claims without validation. Incomplete claims with missing Consultant Codes and illegible receipts enter as "Pending," causing costly operational back-and-forth and weeks of delays.

Agent Knowledge Gaps

Support agents handling 50+ calls/day face high cognitive load. To answer complex queries, they search static Excel sheets or physically consult team leads, inflating Average Handling Time.

Manual Triage Bottleneck

High-volume, low-complexity claims are mixed with complex, high-risk claims. Human adjusters spend time on routine data entry rather than adjudication of genuinely complex cases.

Our Solution

The Unified Intelligence Ecosystem

A central Agentic RAG brain powering three operational layers

The Laya Unified Intelligence architecture creates a single "Master Brain" using Retrieval-Augmented Generation. This central intelligence layer ingests all policy documents, claims history, and operational knowledge to serve as the single source of truth across every touchpoint.

A

Module A

The "Gatekeeper"

Pre-claim validation via WhatsApp & Web that catches incomplete submissions before they enter the system.

  • OCR-powered receipt scanning (95%+ accuracy)
  • Real-time policy & rule validation
  • Interactive feedback for missing data
  • Only "clean" claims reach the backend
B

Module B

The "Copilot"

Real-time agent assist that listens, understands, and surfaces the right answers during live calls.

  • Live voice transcription (<2s latency)
  • Intent recognition for call topics
  • Dynamic "Smart Cards" on agent screen
  • Source citations for compliance
C

Module C

The "Triage Engine"

AI-driven risk scoring and intelligent routing that automates routine claims and flags anomalies.

  • Risk Score (0-100) for every claim
  • Auto-pay for low-risk (Score 0-20)
  • Deep rule check for medium risk
  • Fraud detection with anomaly alerts

Implementation

Phased Roadmap

From MVP to full ecosystem in 16 weeks

Phase 1
Weeks 1-4

The "Smart Search" (MVP)

Solve the Agent knowledge problem first.

  • Ingest Excel Q/A & Policy Docs into Vector DB
  • Launch RAG Chatbot for agents
  • Agents stop using physical "Query Room"
Phase 2
Weeks 5-10

The "Risk Gatekeeper"

Solve the 35k monthly volume bottleneck.

  • Deploy OCR pipeline for incoming scans
  • Build & train Risk Scoring Model
  • Connect low-risk claims to RPA auto-pay
Phase 3
Weeks 11-16

The "Unified Ecosystem"

Full end-to-end integration achieved.

  • Launch WhatsApp Gatekeeper bot
  • Enable Live Voice Transcription
  • Complete unified intelligence loop
